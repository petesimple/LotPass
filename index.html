LotPass

Beta parking passes powered by QR codes, Google Sheets, and a tiny receipt printer with big clipboard energy.

LotPass is still in beta. Parking is only valid after staff confirms payment and prints a dashboard pass.
🚗 Get Parking Pass Enter your plate, then go inside to pay and pick up your printed dashboard pass. 🧾 Staff Admin Create passes, mark paid, print, reprint, and void passes. ✅ Verify Pass Check whether a printed parking pass is valid, paid, and printed.

How it works

1. Scan the parking QR.
2. Enter your license plate and vehicle info.
3. Pay inside with staff.
4. Put the printed pass on your dashboard.
5. Staff can scan the printed QR to verify it.

LotPass beta. Please be patient with the tiny parking robot.
