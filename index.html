Beta 2 parking robot

LotPass

A simple parking pass system for QR code signups, staff verified payment, Google Sheets logging, and T88 dashboard passes.

⚠️
Still in beta. Parking is only valid after staff confirms payment and prints a dashboard pass.
🚗
›
Get Parking Pass Enter your plate and vehicle info, then go inside to pay and pick up your printed dash pass.
🧾
›
Staff Admin Create passes, mark paid, print, reprint, and void parking passes.
✅
›
Verify Pass Check whether a printed parking pass is valid, paid, and printed.

How it works

1

Customer scans the parking QR.

2

They enter plate and vehicle info.

3

Staff confirms payment inside.

4

The T88 prints a dashboard pass.

5

The printed QR verifies against the Sheet.

Powered by Google Sheets, GitHub Pages, Raspberry Pi, and a suspicious amount of confidence.
