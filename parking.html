LotPass

Scan. Submit. Pay inside if needed. Get your parking logged.

Daytime parking is free.
Paid parking starts at 6:00 PM Central and ends at 2:00 AM the next day. If you are parking before 6:00 PM, submit your vehicle info and you are good until the paid parking window begins.
During paid parking hours, your parking is not valid until payment is confirmed and a printed pass is placed on your dashboard.
